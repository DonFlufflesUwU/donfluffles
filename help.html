How to use!!

Navigate to website: To navigate to a website, click the address bar at the top. Type in https:// or http:// followed by your desired domain and then press enter.

Return to previous website:Press the back button (<-) to return to the previous page.

Go forward through pages: Press the forward button (->) to go forward through pages already visited.

Check the URL of a hyperlink: Hover over the link with your mouse. On the bottom of the screen, the status bar will show the exact URL it points to.

See if a page is done loading: Look at the status bar at the bottom of the window. If it says "LOADING..." then the page is still loading. If it says "COMPLETE" then the page has loaded successfully.
